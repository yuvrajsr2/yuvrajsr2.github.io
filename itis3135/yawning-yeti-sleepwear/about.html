About Us

Yawning Yeti Sleepwear started with a simple idea: bedtime should be as fun as it is comfortable. Inspired by the legendary Yeti, who is always on the hunt for warmth and coziness, we designed a line of sleepwear that makes nights feel mythical.

From fuzzy pajamas and oversized hoodies to playful slippers and themed accessories, our mission is to help you sleep like a legend. Whether you're lounging after a long day, gearing up for a sleepover, or just want to add some magic to your nighttime routine, Yawning Yeti Sleepwear is here for you.

Legend says the Yawning Yeti roams snowy mountains searching for the perfect pajamas… but thanks to us, you won’t have to go that far.

“Sleep like a legend.”
