  • Overall Design: Spacing and layout could improve using consistent margins and padding. A max-width wrapper is recommended to prevent overly wide paragraphs.
  • Navigation: Present on all pages, but styling and spacing could be improved.
  • Accessibility: Some images lack alt text and should be updated.
